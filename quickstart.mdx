This guide walks you through everything you need to do to run your first resume analysis — from opening the app to reading your results. The entire process takes less than a minute, and no account setup is required to get started.

<Note>
  Analysis typically completes in just a few seconds. Longer resumes or resumes with complex formatting may take slightly more time, but you will rarely wait more than 30 seconds for your report.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the App">
    Navigate to AI Resume Analyzer in your web browser. The tool runs entirely in the browser — there is nothing to install or configure. You will land directly on the upload screen, ready to begin.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Upload Your Resume">
    Upload your resume file using either the drag-and-drop zone or the **Browse Files** button. AI Resume Analyzer supports the two most common resume formats:

    - **PDF** — recommended for the best parsing accuracy
    - **DOCX** — Microsoft Word documents are fully supported

    For optimal results, keep your file under **5 MB**. Most standard resumes are well within this limit. Avoid password-protected files, as the tool cannot extract text from locked documents.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Your Analysis Type">
    Select the type of analysis you want to run. AI Resume Analyzer offers two modes depending on your goal:

    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="General Analysis">
        **General Analysis** evaluates your resume on its own merits — no job description needed. The AI assesses your resume's overall quality, clarity, structure, and impact. You will receive a holistic score along with specific improvement recommendations covering content, formatting, and ATS readiness.

        Choose General Analysis when you want a thorough review of your resume without targeting a specific role.
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Job Match Analysis">
        **Job Match Analysis** compares your resume directly against a specific job posting. After uploading your resume, paste the full text of the job description into the provided text field.

        The AI will calculate a compatibility match score, highlight the skills and qualifications you already meet, and identify the gaps you need to close to become a stronger candidate for that exact role.

        <Tip>Copy the complete job description — including the responsibilities and requirements sections — for the most accurate match score.</Tip>
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Analyze">
    Once your resume is uploaded and your analysis type is selected, click the **Analyze** button to start the process. The AI will immediately begin reading and evaluating your resume. A progress indicator will keep you informed while the analysis runs — typically just a few seconds.

    Avoid closing or refreshing the page while analysis is in progress.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review Your Report">
    When the analysis is complete, your full report appears on screen. Your report includes:

    - An **overall resume score** with a breakdown by category
    - **Strengths** — what your resume does well
    - **Improvement suggestions** — prioritized, actionable recommendations
    - **Skill gaps** — missing or underrepresented skills (Job Match Analysis)
    - **Course recommendations** — targeted learning to close identified gaps
    - **ATS optimization tips** — keyword and formatting guidance

    For a detailed walkthrough of every section in your report, see [Reading Your Report](/guides/reading-your-report).
  </Step>
</Steps>
