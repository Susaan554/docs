This guide walks you through everything you need to prepare and upload your resume to AI Resume Analyzer. Following these steps ensures the AI extracts your content accurately and delivers the most useful analysis possible.

## Supported File Formats

| Format | Max Size | Notes |
|--------|----------|-------|
| PDF (`.pdf`) | 10 MB | Recommended. Text-based PDFs yield the highest parsing accuracy. |
| DOCX (`.docx`) | 10 MB | Fully supported. Ensure the file is not password-protected. |

## Preparing Your Resume

Before you upload, take a moment to make sure your file is in the best possible shape.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Save your resume as PDF or DOCX">
    Export or save your resume in either PDF or DOCX format. PDF is recommended because it preserves formatting and produces the most reliable text extraction.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ensure text is selectable">
    Open your PDF and try to highlight a word. If you can select text, you have a text-based PDF — this gives you the best results. Scanned image PDFs are supported via OCR fallback, but OCR may introduce minor inaccuracies.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Keep file size reasonable">
    Very large files with many embedded images or graphics can slow down processing. Aim for a clean, content-focused document under 5 MB for the fastest analysis.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Uploading Your File

Once your resume file is ready, uploading it takes only a few seconds.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open AI Resume Analyzer in your browser">
    Navigate to the AI Resume Analyzer application. No account is required to begin an analysis.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the upload area or drag and drop your file">
    On the home screen, click the upload zone or drag your resume file directly onto it. Both PDF and DOCX files are accepted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the correct file is selected">
    After selecting a file, the upload zone displays your file name. Verify it shows the resume you intend to analyze before continuing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Analyze to begin">
    Click the **Analyze** button to submit your resume. The AI will begin processing immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Happens After Upload

Once you click **Analyze**, AI Resume Analyzer extracts all text from your document — whether it's a text-based PDF, DOCX, or a scanned image processed through OCR. The extracted content is then passed to the Gemini AI model, which reads your resume in full and begins generating your personalized analysis. Within moments, your report is ready to review.

<Warning>
  Do not upload resumes containing sensitive personal information beyond what is standard — such as your Social Security Number (SSN), passport number, or financial details — unless you are fully comfortable with that information being processed by the AI.
</Warning>

<Tip>
  Use a clean, well-structured resume template for the best parsing results. Avoid heavy graphics, decorative tables, or multi-column layouts — these can confuse text extraction and reduce the accuracy of your analysis.
</Tip>
