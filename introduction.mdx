AI Resume Analyzer takes the guesswork out of job applications. Whether you are polishing an existing resume, pivoting to a new career, or screening candidates at scale, the tool gives you clear, data-backed feedback powered by Google Generative AI. Upload your resume, optionally paste a job description, and receive a detailed report that tells you exactly what is working, what is missing, and how to improve.

## What is AI Resume Analyzer?

AI Resume Analyzer is an AI-powered evaluation tool that reads your resume the same way a seasoned recruiter would — only faster and without bias. It extracts your skills, experience, education, and accomplishments, then scores your resume across multiple dimensions. When you provide a target job description, it also calculates a compatibility match score and highlights the gaps standing between you and that role.

The tool does not just score your resume; it explains its reasoning. You get prioritized improvement tips, specific course recommendations to close skill gaps, and suggestions to make your resume more readable by Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS).
