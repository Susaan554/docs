Upload your resume and get an instant, in-depth evaluation — including a breakdown of strengths and weaknesses, skill gap analysis, job compatibility scoring, and personalized course recommendations. Whether you're actively job hunting or polishing your resume for future opportunities, AI Resume Analyzer gives you the feedback you need to stand out.

## Get Started in Three Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload Your Resume">
    Upload your resume as a PDF or DOCX file. AI Resume Analyzer extracts and parses the content automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Your Analysis Mode">
    Run a **General Analysis** for an all-around evaluation, or add a job description to get a **Job Match Score** tailored to that role.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Your Report">
    Read your personalized report with scores, skill gaps, strengths, weaknesses, and recommended courses to advance your career.
  </Step>
</Steps>
